TOP WOMEN CRICKETERS IN THE WORLD

1. Ellyse Perry

Ellyse is the world’s best and the youngest cricketer from Australia. She is the youngest Australian cricket player in the women. She plays international cricket where she is the first one to appear in the both ICC ad FIFA World Cup. Ellyse is the first one to appear for both the tournaments in line at the age of 16. She is the one who got recognisation at a very young age. She also holds a lot of individual awards and various tournaments. Ellyse has won the Belinda Clark Awards three times and the Rachael Heyhoe Flint Award not once but twice. Not only this she has her name in Wisden’s Five Cricketers of the Decade 2010-2019. Undoubtedly she is one of the best women cricketers. .

2.Sarah Jane Taylor

Sara is from England bagging the top two positions for the best women’s cricketer in the world. She is popular for her free-flow stroke play. Sara has her best role in retaining the Ashes in the Australi 2008. With her success in the tournament, the cricketer turns out to be the most successful and the popular young cricketer in females. She owns the honor of T20 international female cricketer of 2013.

3.Jhulan Goswami

Jhulan Goswami is from India and ranks in the top 3 positions for the best female cricketer in the world. She belongs to Bengal and is one of the fast bowlers in India. She was the first one to take the 200 wickets in WODIs. Not only this she wins the ICC Women’s Player of the year award in the year 2017. In the year 2011, she bags the M.A. Chidambaram Trophy for the Best Women’s Cricketers. In 2016 she comes to rank 1 of the ICC Women’s ODI bowling rankings. She has got the commendable bowling skills and she holds the position on the top female cricketers in the World.

4. Katherine Helen Brun

Katherine is from England where she is the fastest female bowler in the cricket game. She is also named as the England women’s cricketer of the Year in 2006 and again in 2010. She wins the ICC Women’s T20 World Cup 2009 with England. In the T20 World Cup Final at the Lord’s she bags the man of the match with the glorious score of 3/6 in just 4 overs.
